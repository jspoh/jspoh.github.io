3SG Poh Jing Seng is cool and level-headed, he is able to perceive projects and solve them quickly without panic. This is shown when he can calmly handle operations in the Chong Pang guardroom during adverse times where others would just submit to the stress, 3SG Poh Jing Seng holds his ground calmly and with utmost professionalism.


3SG Poh Jing Seng also has the ability to build great relationships with the people he works with. His friendly and approachable personality has allowed for more open communication and mutual agreements between the troopers and the command team. He leads his troopers confidently with respect and compassion much alike a servant leader rather than leadership through fear and rank superiority. This allows him to work with his troopers without trouble as trust that he has their best in mind.


3SG Poh Jing Seng is also a good team player. He manages platoon 3 shift 1's offs and leaves as well as their detailing carefully and meticulously keeping in mind to evenly distribute the manpower and workload. While not on duty, he often offers himself to the task when help is needed. For example to fill in for troopers when they are not feeling well or if the operation requires more people on the ground to assist.


3SG Poh Jing Seng's commitment and dedication to serving the squadron is also extremely commendable. He has been seen coming back during his off days to support the squadron during emergency landing situations. He will not hesitate to contribute to the platoon in whatever way he can. Having him as a commander in the squadron has been invaluable. Inconclusion, 3SG Poh Jing Seng is a versatile and all-rounded commander who was a vital team player in his platoon during his service and will be an asset to any organisation he belongs to.
